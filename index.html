Introducing Local Link

At LocalLink, we are committed to creating a more connected and sustainable community. Our platform offers two distinct modes to cater to the needs of our users:

Community Mode

In Community Mode, LocalLink empowers citizens to actively engage with their local issues. If you encounter a problem in your neighborhood, such as potholes, broken streetlights, or any other public concern, you can report it directly on our platform. Here’s how it works:

  • Issues: Choose from list of problems provided by us.
  • Engage the Community: Share the report with your neighbors and encourage them to support it.
  • Vote and Upvote: Citizens can upvote the issues they find important. Once an issue reaches 5,000 upvotes, it is escalated to the municipal corporation for official action.

This feature ensures that community concerns are addressed efficiently and that collective voices are heard by local authorities.

Consumer Mode

Consumer Mode is designed to simplify the process of booking reliable local service providers. Whether you need an electrician, plumber, carpenter, or any other skilled worker, LocalLink has you covered. Here’s how it benefits you:

  • Book Local Services: Easily find and book trusted professionals for various home services through our platform.
  • Smart Contracts: We utilize smart contracts to ensure that service providers remain committed to their obligations. This feature helps maintain high service standards and accountability.
  • Secure Transactions: Our platform provides secure payment options, making the process seamless and safe for both consumers and service providers.

With Consumer Mode, you can confidently manage home services and ensure that you receive quality service from reliable providers.

Join LocalLink today and be part of a community-focused platform where your involvement helps improve your neighborhood and access quality services with ease.
